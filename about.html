About Us / 关于我们

MentorWay (明途教育) is co-founded by Yuhan Luo, a Columbia University ERM graduate, and Professor Danielle Taana Smith, a 20+ year veteran in higher education consulting and global development. We are committed to delivering professional, data-supported consulting services to students worldwide.

MentorWay（明途教育）由哥伦比亚大学风险管理硕士 Yuhan Luo 与雪城大学教授 Danielle Taana Smith 联合创办，致力于为学生提供专业、数据驱动的教育咨询服务。
